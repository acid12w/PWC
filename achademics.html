Departments

Business

The Business Education Department at Pathway College equips students with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience needed to succeed in today’s dynamic and competitive business environment. The department blends core business principles with educational theory, preparing graduates for careers in business teaching, corporate training, entrepreneurship, and administration through its Associate Degree programme and Certificate courses

Through a curriculum that covers accounting, management, marketing, economics, and business communication, students gain a strong foundation in business concepts while also learning how to effectively teach and train others. With an emphasis on real-world application, ethical leadership, and technological proficiency, the department fosters critical thinking, problem-solving, and professional development.

Students benefit from experienced faculty, internship opportunities, and strong industry connections, ensuring they are well-prepared to educate, innovate, and lead in both educational and business settings.

Education

The Education Department at Pathway Way College is committed to developing the next generation of educators, literacy leaders, and advocates who are equipped to make a meaningful impact in diverse educational settings. Rooted in a foundation of research-based practices, equity, and lifelong learning, the department offers a comprehensive programme that prepare students for a career in teaching, educational leadership, and related fields.

Through a blend of theoretical coursework, practical field experiences, and strong partnerships with local schools, our students will gain the knowledge, skills, and real-world insight necessary to support learners in the classroom and inspire them to become model citizens. The department emphasizes inclusive education, reflective practice, and the integration of technology in modern classrooms.

Students in the Education Department are prepared to lead with integrity, adaptability, and a passion for learning.

Mathematics and Science

The Mathematics and Science Department at Pathway College serves as one of the cornerstones of the institution’s academic foundation, providing students with the knowledge and skills essential for success in an increasingly complex and technology-driven world.

Combining the disciplines of mathematics, biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science, the department aims to foster critical thinking, problem-solving, and analytical reasoning through a rigorous online teacher training programme in a supportive learning environment.

Legal Studies

The Legal Studies Department at Pathway College offers a comprehensive and interdisciplinary approach to the study of law, justice, and legal systems. Designed to prepare students for careers in law, public service, compliance, and related fields, the program emphasizes critical thinking, ethical reasoning, and a deep understanding of legal principles in both national and global contexts.

Whether students are planning to attend law school, work in the legal field, or pursue roles in government or advocacy, the Legal Studies Department equips them with the analytical skills and legal knowledge necessary to navigate complex legal and policy issues with confidence and integrity.
