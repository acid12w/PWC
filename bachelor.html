Bachelor’s Degree

A Bachelor’s Degree in Reading and English Language Education

The Bachelor’s Degree is a four year programme and all applicants must meet the entry requirements to be accepted into the programme.

Entry Requirements :

Five (5) CSEC subjects including English Language and English Literature at Grade One, Two, or Three. A Grade One or Two pass in Social Studies is accepted if applicants do not have a pass in English Literature.
